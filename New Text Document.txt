Sri Lanka is slowly recovering from a severe economic crisis while growth remains slow and inflation is on the rise. 
With fiscal challenges and risks from debt, the economic stability of the country is fragile.
While a country with a wealth of natural resources and a diverse economy based in agriculture, mining, fishing, tourism and manufacturing, Sri Lanka is truly a “A Land Like No Other”
So harnessing technology like Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and statistical tools can significantly contribute to the economic stability.
